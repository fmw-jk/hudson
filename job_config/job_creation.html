Jenkins job creation

With this form you can create multiple Jenkins jobs in an easy way. Jenkins will build your forks of the selected stacks every night and after each modification.
In the unlikely case of any problems while building your code, Jenkins will inform you via an email. Thus you will always know whether your code is working or not.
